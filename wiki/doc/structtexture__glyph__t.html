texture_glyph_t Struct Reference
Texture font

Detailed Description

A structure that describe a glyph.

Examples:
demo-atb-agg.c, demo-benchmark.c, demo-cartoon.c, demo-console.c, demo-display-list.c, demo-font.c, demo-makefont.c, and demo-outline.c.

int texture_glyph_t::offset_y

Glyphs's top bearing expressed in integer pixels.

Remember that this is the distance from the baseline to the top-most glyph scanline, upwards y coordinates being positive.

Examples:
demo-atb-agg.c, demo-benchmark.c, demo-cartoon.c, demo-console.c, demo-display-list.c, demo-font.c, demo-makefont.c, and demo-outline.c.
