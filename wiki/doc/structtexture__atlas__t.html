Texture atlas

Detailed Description

A texture atlas is used to pack several small regions into a single texture.

Examples:
demo-atb-agg.c, demo-benchmark.c, demo-cartoon.c, demo-console.c, demo-display-list.c, demo-distance-field.c, demo-font.c, demo-outline.c, and demo-texture.c.
