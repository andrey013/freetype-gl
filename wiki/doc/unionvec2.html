vec2 Union Reference

Detailed Description

Tuple of 2 floats.

Each field can be addressed using several aliases:

  • First component: x or s
  • Second component: y or t
Examples:
demo-ansi.c, demo-atb-agg.c, demo-benchmark.c, demo-cartoon.c, demo-console.c, demo-font.c, demo-gamma.c, demo-markup.c, demo-outline.c, and demo-subpixel.c.
